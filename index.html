🌈 About Larissa – Creator of Speak Louder, Love Harder

I’m Larissa Wood, and I built Speak Louder, Love Harder from scratch — not just as a website, but as a safe space for people like me.

This isn’t a brand. This is personal.

I’ve lived through anxiety, exhaustion, burnout, and survival mode. I’ve had to learn how to take care of my mental and physical health with limited resources, no roadmap, and no one handing me solutions. Everything I’ve built — from the mood tracker to the affirmations, the journal to the guestbook — came from my own healing journey.

I’m self-taught in design, coding, and coping. I made this site by myself, piece by piece, with the goal of creating what I wish I had when I was struggling most:
A space that feels warm, honest, and supportive — where you don’t have to pretend to be okay to belong.

“I am mine before I am anyone else’s. Before the world asks me to shrink or be softer or quieter — I choose to belong to myself.”

I’m not here to fix you. I’m just here to remind you that you’re not alone, you’re not broken, and you’re allowed to take up space — especially when it’s hard.

Thanks for being here. Keep going. Keep feeling.
And always, speak louder and love harder — especially toward yourself.
